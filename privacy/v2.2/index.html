Privacy Policy for SixFour Chess

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Publisher: ADOUANI SAS

Application: SixFour - Chess Game (iOS)

Select Language: English Français Italiano

Overview

SixFour is an iPhone chess application focused on local play against the AI. This version of the app no longer offers online multiplayer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and clearly explaining what stays on your device and what may use the network.

Data Controller

ADOUANI SAS

Email: adouani.sas@gmail.com

What stays on your device

Most of SixFour works entirely on your device. The app stores locally only:

  • Your current game state
  • Your local history of finished games
  • Your move-by-move replay data
  • Your chosen difficulty and preferences
  • Your settings for sound and in-game coaching messages

This local data is not uploaded to our servers.

What may use the network

The app may use the network only in limited situations:

  • Master difficulty: chess positions in FEN format may be sent to analysis services to compute a stronger AI move.
  • Privacy preferences: if optional analytics or crash reporting are enabled in a future release, they will be described here before any activation.

Cloud analysis for Master difficulty

When you choose Master difficulty, SixFour may send a chess position in FEN format to external analysis providers in order to compute the AI move.

  • No account is required
  • No email address is sent
  • No name, contacts, photos, or location data are sent
  • The transmitted content is limited to chess position data needed for analysis

Third-party services

Cloud analysis providers

For Master difficulty only, SixFour may contact:

  • lichess.org
  • stockfish.online

These services receive chess position data in order to calculate a move. Their own privacy policies apply to the processing they perform.

No advertising services

SixFour does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Online multiplayer or matchmaking services

Permissions

SixFour does not request access to sensitive device features.

  • Network access: only for Master difficulty analysis and website links
  • No location access
  • No camera access
  • No microphone access
  • No photo library access
  • No contacts access

Data security

Your local data benefits from iOS application sandboxing and device-level protections. Network exchanges used for Master difficulty rely on HTTPS connections provided by the third-party services involved.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you may contact us for any question related to this policy or to any processing under our responsibility.

Access and deletion

  • Local data: you can remove it by uninstalling the app.
  • Questions or requests: contact us at adouani.sas@gmail.com.

Response time

We aim to respond within 30 days.

Privacy preferences

SixFour includes privacy controls in the app. These preferences are stored locally on your device and applied immediately.

The app is currently centered on local play. There is no online account, no matchmaking, and no player profile hosted by us.

Legal basis

  • Local gameplay and storage: legitimate interest in providing the core functionality of the app.
  • Master difficulty network analysis: necessary to provide the feature you explicitly choose to use.

Retention period

Data Type Retention Period
Current game, history, preferences Until deletion by you or app uninstall
Master difficulty position requests Not retained by us

Children's privacy

SixFour can be used by players of all ages. Because the app primarily works locally and does not require an account, personal data exposure is limited.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The date shown at the top of the page indicates the latest revision.
